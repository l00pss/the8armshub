## Why Go?

We chose Go as our implementation language because it excels across all our domains:

### **For Algorithms**
- Clean syntax that doesn't obscure logic
- Excellent performance for computational tasks
- Strong typing catches errors early

### **For Mathematics** 
- Powerful standard library for numerical computing
- Concurrency for parallel mathematical operations
- Growing ecosystem of mathematical libraries

### **For Distributed Systems**
- Built-in concurrency with goroutines and channels
- Excellent networking and HTTP support
- Used by major distributed systems (Docker, Kubernetes, etcd)
